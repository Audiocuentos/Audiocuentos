Benny Busy Day

Versión en Inglés

Title: Benny Busy Day

Once upon a time, in a small cozy house, lived a little boy named Benny. Benny was a cheerful and curious boy who loved to explore and play. Every day, he had a special routine that he followed with joy.

Benny's day started early in the morning when the sun was just waking up. His mom would come to his room and gently say, "Good morning, Benny! It's time to wake up." Benny would stretch his arms and smile at the bright sunlight streaming through his window.

First, Benny would brush his teeth. He had a blue toothbrush with a little dinosaur on it. Benny loved dinosaurs! He would squeeze a small amount of toothpaste onto his toothbrush and brush his teeth until they were sparkling clean.

After brushing his teeth, it was time for breakfast. Benny's mom always made delicious breakfasts. Sometimes it was pancakes with syrup, other times it was scrambled eggs with toast. Benny's favorite was a big bowl of oatmeal with fresh berries on top. He would sit at the kitchen table, swinging his legs happily as he ate.

Once breakfast was over, Benny would get dressed. He had a drawer full of colorful clothes. Benny loved to wear his red shirt with a yellow star on it. He thought it made him look like a superhero. After getting dressed, Benny was ready for his favorite part of the day – playtime!

Benny had a big backyard with a swing set and a sandbox. He loved to swing high up into the sky, feeling the wind rush through his hair. Sometimes, he pretended he was flying like a bird. Other times, he would build sandcastles in the sandbox, imagining he was a king in a grand castle.

After playing outside, it was time for a little learning. Benny's mom would read him stories and teach him new things. Benny loved storytime. He had a big book with colorful pictures and exciting tales about animals, adventures, and faraway lands.

Lunchtime came next. Benny always looked forward to lunch because he got to eat his favorite foods. Today, it was a yummy sandwich with cheese and ham, a crunchy apple, and a glass of milk.

In the afternoon, Benny would take a short nap. He would cuddle up with his favorite teddy bear, Mr. Fluffy, and drift off to sleep. When he woke up, it was time for more play and fun activities until dinner.

Dinner was a special family time. Benny, his mom, and dad would sit together and talk about their day. Benny loved listening to his parents' stories and sharing his own.
After dinner, Benny would take a warm bath. He loved playing with his rubber duck and making bubbles. Then, it was time to put on his pajamas and brush his teeth again before bed.

Finally, Benny's mom would tuck him into bed, read him a bedtime story, and give him a goodnight kiss. "Goodnight, Benny," she would say. "Sleep tight." Benny would close his eyes, thinking about all the fun he had that day, and soon he would drift off to dreamland.

And so, Benny's busy day came to a happy end, and he was ready for more adventures tomorrow.

Versión en Español

Title: El Día Ocupado de Benny

Érase una vez, en una pequeña casa acogedora, vivía un niño llamado Benny. Benny era un niño alegre y curioso al que le encantaba explorar y jugar. Todos los días, tenía una rutina especial que seguía con alegría.

El día de Benny comenzaba temprano en la mañana, cuando el sol apenas despertaba. Su mamá entraba a su habitación y le decía suavemente: "Buenos días, Benny. Es hora de levantarse". Benny estiraba sus brazos y sonreía al ver la luz brillante que entraba por su ventana.

Primero, Benny se cepillaba los dientes. Tenía un cepillo de dientes azul con un pequeño dinosaurio. ¡A Benny le encantaban los dinosaurios! Ponía una pequeña cantidad de pasta de dientes en su cepillo y se cepillaba los dientes hasta dejarlos relucientes.

Después de cepillarse los dientes, era hora del desayuno. La mamá de Benny siempre preparaba desayunos deliciosos. A veces eran panqueques con jarabe y otras veces eran huevos revueltos con tostadas. El favorito de Benny era un gran tazón de avena con frutas frescas encima. Se sentaba en la mesa de la cocina, balanceando felizmente sus piernas mientras comía.

Una vez que terminaba el desayuno, Benny se vestía. Tenía un cajón lleno de ropa colorida. A Benny le encantaba ponerse su camiseta roja con una estrella amarilla. Pensaba que lo hacía parecer un superhéroe. Después de vestirse, Benny estaba listo para su parte favorita del día: ¡la hora de jugar!

Benny tenía un gran patio trasero con un columpio y una caja de arena. Le encantaba columpiarse alto en el cielo, sintiendo el viento en su cabello. A veces, imaginaba que volaba como un pájaro. Otras veces, construía castillos de arena en la caja de arena, imaginando que era un rey en un gran castillo.

Después de jugar afuera, era hora de aprender un poco. La mamá de Benny le leía cuentos y le enseñaba cosas nuevas. A Benny le encantaba la hora de los cuentos. Tenía un gran libro con imágenes coloridas y emocionantes historias sobre animales, aventuras y tierras lejanas.

Luego venía la hora del almuerzo. Benny siempre esperaba con ansias el almuerzo porque podía comer sus comidas favoritas. Hoy, era un delicioso sándwich de queso y jamón, una manzana crujiente y un vaso de leche.

Por la tarde, Benny tomaba una siesta corta. Se acurrucaba con su osito de peluche favorito, el señor Fluffy, y se dormía. Cuando se despertaba, era hora de más juegos y actividades divertidas hasta la cena.

La cena era un momento especial en familia. Benny, su mamá y su papá se sentaban juntos y hablaban sobre su día. A Benny le encantaba escuchar las historias de sus padres y compartir las suyas.

Después de la cena, Benny se bañaba con agua caliente. Le encantaba jugar con su patito de goma y hacer burbujas. Luego, era hora de ponerse el pijama y cepillarse los dientes otra vez antes de dormir.

Finalmente, la mamá de Benny lo arropaba en la cama, le leía un cuento y le daba un beso de buenas noches. "Buenas noches, Benny," le decía. "Que duermas bien." Benny cerraba los ojos, pensando en toda la diversión que había tenido ese día, y pronto se dormía.

Y así, el día ocupado de Benny llegaba a un feliz final, y él estaba listo para más aventuras mañana.
