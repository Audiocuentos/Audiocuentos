Versión en Inglés

Title: Tommy's Wonderful Day


Tommy was a little boy who lived in a small town. Every morning, he woke up early with a big smile on his face. He loved his morning routine because it always started with his favorite breakfast.

First, Tommy would brush his teeth. He used his blue toothbrush and minty toothpaste. He loved how fresh his mouth felt afterward. Then, he washed his face with warm water. This made him feel awake and ready for the day.

Next, Tommy went to the kitchen where his mom was making breakfast. His favorite meal was pancakes with syrup and strawberries. Today was special because his mom made extra pancakes just for him. Tommy sat at the table and ate his delicious breakfast. He always made sure to thank his mom for the tasty food.

After breakfast, Tommy got dressed. He picked out his favorite red shirt and blue jeans. He loved these clothes because they made him feel like a superhero. Once he was dressed, he put on his shoes and grabbed his backpack.

Tommy's next stop was school. He loved school because he got to see his friends and learn new things. His mom walked with him to the bus stop. The yellow school bus arrived, and Tommy hopped on, waving goodbye to his mom.

At school, Tommy's first class was math. He liked math because he enjoyed solving puzzles. After math, it was time for recess. Tommy and his friends played tag on the playground. He loved running around and laughing with his friends.

After recess, Tommy had a reading class. His teacher read a story about a brave knight and a dragon. Tommy loved listening to stories and imagining himself as the knight.

Lunchtime was next. Tommy ate a sandwich, an apple, and a cookie. He always shared his cookie with his best friend, Sarah. They talked about their favorite games and laughed together.

In the afternoon, Tommy had art class. He loved drawing and painting. Today, he painted a picture of a sunny day with a rainbow. His teacher said his painting was very colorful and beautiful. Tommy felt proud.

After school, Tommy took the bus back home. He told his mom all about his day. Then, he did his homework at the kitchen table. His mom helped him with the hard problems. When he finished, he played with his toys until dinner time.

For dinner, Tommy's family had spaghetti and meatballs. It was one of Tommy's favorite dinners. After dinner, they all watched a movie together. Tommy loved spending time with his family.

Finally, it was time for bed. Tommy put on his pajamas and brushed his teeth again. His mom read him a bedtime story about a pirate adventure. Tommy loved hearing about the pirate's exciting journey.

As Tommy lay in bed, he thought about his wonderful day. He was happy and felt loved. He closed his eyes and dreamed about all the fun he would have tomorrow.

And so, Tommy fell asleep, ready for another wonderful day.

Title: El Maravilloso Día de Tommy


Tommy era un niño que vivía en un pequeño pueblo. Cada mañana, se despertaba temprano con una gran sonrisa en su cara. Le encantaba su rutina matutina porque siempre comenzaba con su desayuno favorito.

Primero, Tommy se cepillaba los dientes. Usaba su cepillo de dientes azul y pasta de dientes de menta. Le encantaba lo fresca que se sentía su boca después. Luego, se lavaba la cara con agua tibia. Esto lo hacía sentir despierto y listo para el día.

Después, Tommy iba a la cocina donde su mamá estaba preparando el desayuno. Su comida favorita eran los panqueques con jarabe y fresas. Hoy era un día especial porque su mamá hizo panqueques extra solo para él. Tommy se sentó a la mesa y comió su delicioso desayuno. Siempre se aseguraba de agradecerle a su mamá por la comida sabrosa.

Después del desayuno, Tommy se vestía. Elegía su camiseta roja favorita y sus jeans azules. Le encantaba esta ropa porque lo hacía sentir como un superhéroe. Una vez vestido, se ponía los zapatos y agarraba su mochila.

El siguiente destino de Tommy era la escuela. Le encantaba la escuela porque podía ver a sus amigos y aprender cosas nuevas. Su mamá lo acompañaba hasta la parada del autobús. El autobús escolar amarillo llegaba, y Tommy subía de un salto, despidiéndose de su mamá con la mano.

En la escuela, la primera clase de Tommy era matemáticas. Le gustaban las matemáticas porque disfrutaba resolviendo acertijos. Después de matemáticas, era hora del recreo. Tommy y sus amigos jugaban a las atrapadas en el patio de juegos. Le encantaba correr y reír con sus amigos.

Después del recreo, Tommy tenía clase de lectura. Su maestro leyó una historia sobre un valiente caballero y un dragón. A Tommy le encantaba escuchar historias e imaginarse a sí mismo como el caballero.

La hora del almuerzo fue la siguiente. Tommy comió un sándwich, una manzana y una galleta. Siempre compartía su galleta con su mejor amiga, Sarah. Hablaban sobre sus juegos favoritos y reían juntos.

Por la tarde, Tommy tenía clase de arte. Le encantaba dibujar y pintar. Hoy, pintó un cuadro de un día soleado con un arcoíris. Su maestro dijo que su pintura era muy colorida y hermosa. Tommy se sintió orgulloso.

Después de la escuela, Tommy tomó el autobús de regreso a casa. Le contó a su mamá todo sobre su día. Luego, hizo su tarea en la mesa de la cocina. Su mamá lo ayudaba con los problemas difíciles. Cuando terminó, jugó con sus juguetes hasta la hora de la cena.

Para la cena, la familia de Tommy comió espaguetis con albóndigas. Era una de las cenas favoritas de Tommy. Después de la cena, todos vieron una película juntos. A Tommy le encantaba pasar tiempo con su familia.

Finalmente, era hora de ir a la cama. Tommy se puso su pijama y se cepilló los dientes de nuevo. Su mamá le leyó una historia para dormir sobre una aventura pirata. A Tommy le encantaba escuchar sobre el emocionante viaje del pirata.

Mientras Tommy se acostaba en la cama, pensaba en su maravilloso día. Estaba feliz y se sentía amado. Cerró los ojos y soñó con toda la diversión que tendría mañana.

Y así, Tommy se quedó dormido, listo para otro día maravilloso.
